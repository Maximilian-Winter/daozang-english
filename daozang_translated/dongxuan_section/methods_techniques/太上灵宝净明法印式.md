# 太上灵宝净明法印式

The *Scripture of the Supreme Numinous Treasure’s Pure Brightness Dharma Seals*
Title: *Scripture of the Supreme Numinous Treasure’s Pure Brightness Dharma Seal Formulas*. Author unknown. Three sections in one scroll.
Source text: *Zhengtong Daoist Canon*, Dongxuan Section, Methods Category.

All who receive the Pure Brightness Dharma shall carve two seals, each two cun and four fen in size. One is called the *Seal of the Supreme Pure Brightness*, the other the *Seal of the Lord of the Pure Brightness Dharma*. The *Pure Brightness Seal* is used for memorials. The *Seal of the Lord of the Dharma* is employed for dispatching summons to ghosts and spirits.

Here ends the *Scripture of the Supreme Numinous Treasure’s Pure Brightness Dharma Seal Formulas*.
