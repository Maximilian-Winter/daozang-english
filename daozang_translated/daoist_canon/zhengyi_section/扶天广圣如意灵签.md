Veiled in verdant luxuriance.
Fair one, I admire you,
And string you as my pendant.

Divining clear or cloudy skies: clear.
Silkworms and crops promising; tombs and burials slightly auspicious.
Livestock unharmed; travelers soon to arrive.
Plans fulfilled late; wealth sought profitable later.
Marriages may succeed; official matters uneventful.
Audiences permitted; journeys should proceed.
Construction should commence; illness no hindrance.
Lost items should be sought; childbirth with alarm.
Thieves gone far; prayers and sacrifices half favorable.
Ominous signs minor calamity; relocation slightly favorable.
Households ultimately auspicious; documents appear late.
**All above moderately inauspicious at first, auspicious in the end,**
Corresponds to the numbers two, four, and eight,
And to the years, months, days, and hours of *yin*, *wu*, and *xu*,
With the direction due south.

**One Hundred Fifteenth**　　**Most Inauspicious**

To know contentment is to avoid disgrace.

To know restraint is to avoid peril.
Without heeding right instruction, one incurs disaster and harm.
Divining clear or overcast skies: clear.

Livestock depleted; travelers obstructed.
Plans unfulfilled; wealth unprofitable.
Marriages fail; official matters unresolved.
Audiences hard to obtain; journeys hindered.
Construction inauspicious; illness difficult to cure.
Lost items hard to recover; childbirth perilous.
Thieves unapprehended; prayers and sacrifices inadvisable.
Ominous signs bring disaster; relocation invites misfortune.
Households unsettled; documents hard to obtain.
**All above auspicious at first, inauspicious in the end,**
Corresponds to the numbers one, five, and seven,
And to the years, months, days, and hours of *hai*, *mao*, and *wei*,
With the direction northeast.

Deprived of foreknowledge, what can a single day avail?
For divining clear or overcast skies: clear.
Silkworms and crops fail; burial ruins the household.
Livestock suffers disaster; travelers do not return.
Plans greatly unfulfilled; wealth greatly unprofitable.
Marriages greatly unmade; official matters most inauspicious.
Audiences unmet; journeys inadvisable.

Thieves not to be sought; prayers and sacrifices invite calamity.
