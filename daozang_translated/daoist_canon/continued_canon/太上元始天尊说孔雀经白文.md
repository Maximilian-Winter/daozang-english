# 太上元始天尊说孔雀经白文

The Most High Primordial Heavenly Worthy expounds the *White Text of the Peacock Sutra*

*Know the chime, arise—*
Heavenly Worthy of Longevity and Life-Preservation,
Heavenly Worthy of Great Compassion, Longevity, and Life-Preservation.

*Enlightened mindfulness—*
Vast and wondrous, the profound and numinous chapter,
Cultivated over ten thousand kalpas by the Peacock King.
The Primordial Beginning, true master of scriptural teaching,
Expounds the wondrous Dharma in broad dissemination.

Heavenly Worthy of Great Compassion, Longevity, and Life-Preservation.
