# 北斗九皇隐讳经

**The Northern Dipper’s Nine Sovereigns’ Esoteric Taboo Scripture**

**Scripture Title:** *The Northern Dipper’s Nine Sovereigns’ Esoteric Taboo Scripture*. The author is unknown, likely composed by Shangqing Daoists of the Northern and Southern Dynasties or the Sui-Tang period. Its content closely aligns with *The Precious Register of the Yellow River’s Chart from the Supreme Purity*. One scroll. Source text: *The Wanli Continuation of the Daoist Canon*.

---

**The Northern Dipper’s Nine Sovereigns’ Esoteric Taboo Scripture**

The *Scripture of the Yellow Emperor and Laozi* states:
The first star of the Northern Dipper, **Tian Shu (Celestial Pivot)**, is the *hun*-spirit of the **Yang Brightness Star**;
The second, **Tian You (Celestial Wanderer)**, is the *hun*-spirit of the **Yin Essence Star**;
The third, **Tian Ji (Celestial Mechanism)**, is the *po*-essence of the **Perfected Person Star**;
The fourth, **Tian Quan (Celestial Balance)**, is the *po*-essence of the **Mysterious Darkness Star**;
The fifth, **Yu Heng (Jade Balance)**, is the *po*-spirit of the **Cinnabar Prime Star**;
The sixth, **Yue Yang (Gate of Yang)**, is the *po*-spirit of the **Northern Culmen Star**;
The seventh, **Yao Guang (Luminous Jade)**, is the *hun*-spirit of the **Celestial Pass Star**, the Great Brilliance;
The eighth, **Dong Ming (Cavernous Brightness)**, is the *hun*-essence of the **Assistant Star**, the Yang Brightness;
The ninth, **Yin Yuan (Hidden Prime)**, is the *hun*-spirit of the **Adjutant Star**, the Luminous Void.

The **Yang Brightness Star** is the Celestial Grand Commandant, governing rectitude and transgressions. Above, it oversees the perfected of the Nine Heavens; in the middle, it supervises the flying immortals of the Five Peaks; below, it commands the adepts of later generations. The merits and demerits of the gods and spirits of Heaven and Earth are all subject to its jurisdiction.

The **Yin Essence Star** is the Celestial Grand Steward, governing official rank and emolument. Above, it oversees the celestial lodges; below, it commands the myriad spirits and those who study immortality. The destiny, rank, and emolument of all who cultivate the Dao and the common people are subject to its jurisdiction.

The **Perfected Person Star** is the Celestial Minister of Works, governing immortals and transcendents. Above, it oversees the lofty perfected of the Nine Heavens; in the middle, it supervises the numinous immortals of the Five Peaks; below, it commands those who study the Dao. The offices of the perfected immortals are all subject to its jurisdiction.

The **Mysterious Darkness Star** is the Celestial Vanguard, governing the punishment of rebels. Above, it oversees the ghosts and spirits of the Nine Heavens; in the middle, it commands the Three Officials of the Northern Thearch; below, it supervises the myriad multitudes, punishing rebels and the disobedient. All who harbor malevolent intentions are subject to its jurisdiction.

The **Cinnabar Prime Star** is the **Lord of the Northern Dipper**, governing the registers of destiny. Above, it oversees the celestial records of the Nine Heavens; in the middle, it administers the ledgers of ghosts and spirits; below, it commands the destiny registers of Daoist adepts and the common people. All in Heaven and Earth are encompassed within its purview.

The **Northern Culmen Star** is the Celestial Grand Master of Ceremonies, governing advancement. Above, it oversees the perfected of the Nine Heavens; in the middle, it commands the flying immortals of the Five Peaks; below, it governs the ranks of scholars. All who ascend through merit and diligence are subject to its authority.

The **Celestial Pass Star** is the Celestial Supreme Thearch, governing the mechanisms of Heaven and Earth—such as the length of the four seasons and the cycles of fortune and calamity—all of which are subject to its jurisdiction.

The **Assistant Star** is the star of the **August Jade Thearch**, called the Eternal Yang, governing flying immortals. Above, it oversees the Nine Heavens; below, it commands the Nine Earths. The offices of the immortals of the Five Peaks and the Four Seas all derive from it.

The **Adjutant Star** is the **Perfected Star of the Grand Master of Ceremonies**, called the Void, ever hidden and recondite, governing boundless transformations.

The *Precious Record of the Yellow River’s Chart* states:
The first, **Yang Brightness Star**, is the Celestial Grand Commandant, governing rectitude and transgressions. Above, it oversees the perfected of the Nine Heavens; in the middle, it supervises the flying immortals of the Five Peaks; below, it commands the perfected of later generations. The merits and demerits of the gods and spirits of Heaven and Earth are all subject to it.
Encompassing nine hundred and twenty *li*, there is a **Jade Tower of the Azure City** where the **Perfected of the Dipper’s Handle**, titled the **Nine Dawns Lord**, resides. His surname is **Upper Numinous**, his taboo name **Shaking the Celestial Mallet**. He wears a **Nine Dawns Jade Crown**, dons a **flying robe of azure feathers**, and holds the **Mysterious Chart of the Dipper**, seated within the jade tower. Those who know his inner taboo are illuminated by the **Jade Dawn** and soar through the Great Void.

The second, **Yin Essence Star**, is the Celestial Grand Steward, governing official rank and emolument. Above, it oversees the celestial lodges; below, it commands the myriad spirits and those who study immortality.
Encompassing five hundred and fifty-five *li*, there is a **Five-Colored Jade Tower** where the **Perfected Clinging to the Dipper’s Bowl**, titled the **Northern Supreme Dawn Lord**, resides. His surname is **Nurturing Infant**, his taboo name **Stimulating Bright Light**. He wears a **Mysterious Essence Jade Crown**, dons a **flying robe of dark feathers**, and holds a **five-colored feathered tally**.

The third, **Perfected Person Star**, is the Celestial Minister of Works, governing immortals and transcendents. Above, it oversees the lofty perfected of the Nine Heavens; in the middle, it supervises the numinous immortals of the Five Peaks; below, it commands those who study the Dao.
Encompassing seven hundred and seventy *li*, there is a **Yellow Terrace Jade Tower** where the **Perfected Treading the Dipper**, titled the **Lord of Immortal Florescence**, resides. His surname is **Returning Jade**, his taboo name **Measuring the Multitudes’ Paths**. He wears a **Flying Dawn Treasure Crown**, dons a **flying robe of azure feathers**, and holds the **Azure Register of the Dipper’s Center**.

The fourth, **Mysterious Darkness Star**, is the Celestial Vanguard, governing the punishment of rebels. Above, it oversees the ghosts and spirits of the Nine Heavens; in the middle, it commands the Three Officials of the Northern Thearch; below, it supervises the myriad multitudes.
Encompassing eight hundred *li*, there is a **Vermilion Terrace Jade Tower** where the **Perfected Pacing the Dipper**, titled the **Mysterious Supreme Flying Canopy Dawn Lord**, resides. His surname is **Dark Pivot**, his taboo name **Shaking the Celestial Pillar**. He wears a **Three Florescence Treasure Dawn Crown**, dons a **flying robe of cinnabar brocade**, and holds the **tally of the Nine Spirits**.

The fifth, **Cinnabar Prime Star**, is the **Lord of the Northern Dipper**, governing the registers of destiny. Above, it oversees the celestial records of the Nine Heavens; in the middle, it administers the ledgers of ghosts and spirits; below, it commands the destiny registers of Daoist adepts and the common people. All in Heaven and Earth are encompassed within its purview.
Encompassing seven hundred and twenty *li*, there is a **White Terrace Golden Tower** where the **Perfected Treading the Dipper’s Traces**, titled the **Seven Dawns Lord of the Golden Dipper**, resides. His surname is **Upper Opening**, his taboo name **Transforming the Five Paths**. He wears a **Seven Treasures Flying Heaven Crown**, dons a **flying robe of white brocade**, and holds the **Azure Prime Register**.

The sixth, **Northern Culmen Star**, is the Celestial Grand Master of Ceremonies, governing advancement. Above, it oversees the perfected of the Nine Heavens; in the middle, it commands the flying immortals of the Five Peaks; below, it governs the ranks of scholars.
Encompassing seven hundred and seventy *li*, there is a **Mysterious Terrace Jade Tower** where the **Perfected Pacing the Firmament**, titled the **Northern Dawn Flying Florescence Lord**, resides. His surname is **Bright Numinous**, his taboo name **Prospering the Upper Prime**. He wears a **Flying Essence Florescence Crown**, dons a **flying robe of purple brocade**, and holds the **Nine Dipper Jade Tablets**.

The seventh, **Celestial Pass Star**, is the Celestial Supreme Thearch, governing the mechanisms of Heaven and Earth—such as the length of the four seasons and the cycles of fortune and calamity.

Encompassing nine hundred *li*, there is a **Nine-Layered Jade Tower** where the **Dragon-Riding Perfected**, titled the **Comprehensive Numinous Nine Prime Northern Canopy Dawn Lord**, resides. His surname is **Mysterious Pivot**, his taboo name **Opening Heaven’s Passage**. He wears a **Nine Prime Treasure Crown**, dons a **nine-hued brocade robe**, and holds the **Radiant Spirit Seal**.

The eighth, **Auxiliary Star**, is the star of the **Celestial Honored Jade Thearch**, also called the **Perpetual Yang**. It governs the flying immortals, overseeing the Nine Heavens above and commanding the Nine Lands below, as well as the officials of immortals across the Five Peaks and Four Marshes.

Encompassing nine hundred and ninety *li*, there is a **Purple Qi Jade Tower** where the **Perfected Roaming the Three Realms**, titled the **Imperial Honored Nine Dawn Lord**, resides. His surname is **Essence of Constancy**, his taboo name **Void Above Opening the Upright Extension**. He wears a **Flying Essence Jade Crown** and a **nine-hued phoenix robe**, and holds a **fire bell**.

The ninth, **Supporting Star**, is the star of the **Grand Thearch Perfected**, also called the **Void Concealment**. It governs boundless transformations.

Encompassing nine hundred and ninety *li*, there is a **Jade Tower and Purple Palace** where the **Perfected Wandering the Three Yangs**, titled the **Imperial Perfected Prime Dawn Lord**, resides. His surname is **Dark Void**, his taboo name **Obscure Yang Radiance of the Profound Desolation**. He wears a **Flying Heaven Jade Crown** and a **Nine Heavens Dragon Robe**, and holds the **Imperial Writ**.

Should you wish to expose another’s wrongs and clarify your own rightness, suppress another’s evil and affirm your own goodness, reproach yourself without resenting others, and seek understanding while praying for blessings—removing transgressions and extending fortune—petition the **First Jade Thearch**, the **Celestial Grand Marshal**.

Should you seek official position and worldly advancement as a student of yin and yang, petition the **Second Jade Thearch**, the **Celestial High Steward**.

Should you study the Dao in pursuit of immortality, commune with spirits, and attain sagehood, petition the **Third Jade Thearch**, the **Celestial Minister of Works**.

Should you subdue malevolent ghosts and spirits, execute the hidden and manifest wicked, petition the **Fourth Jade Thearch**, the **Celestial Vanguard**.

Should you establish merit, cultivate virtue, extend your lifespan, and prolong your years, petition the **Fifth Jade Thearch**, the **Celestial Dipper Lord**.

Should you suffer from affliction and misfortune, beseeching relief and exemption, petition the **Sixth Jade Thearch**, the **Celestial Grand Master of Ceremonies**.

Should the world be in turmoil, with qi and seasons in disorder, petition the **Seventh Jade Thearch**, the **Celestial Supreme Thearch**.

Should you wish to avert myriad calamities, ascend above and tread below, petition the **Eighth Jade Thearch**, the **Celestial Honored Jade Thearch**.

Should you seek boundless transformation to rescue all beings, petition the **Ninth Jade Thearch**, the **Celestial Grand Thearch**.

The hidden surnames and taboo names of the **Nine Thearchs** and **Nine Ladies**—knowing them extends life by a thousand years. Regularly observe them at dusk, envisioning the forms and majestic radiance of the Thearchs and Ladies, recalling their surnames and taboo names, and fixing them firmly in mind. By doing so, you may behold the eighth and ninth stars, attaining limitless longevity.

The *Scripture of the Hidden Taboo Names of the Nine Thearchs of the Northern Dipper* concludes.
