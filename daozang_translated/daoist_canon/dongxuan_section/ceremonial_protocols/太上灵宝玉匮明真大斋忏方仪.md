# 太上灵宝玉匮明真大斋忏方仪

**The Supreme Numinous Treasure Jade Casket Luminous Perfection Great Fast and Repentance Ritual**

**Scripture Title:** *The Supreme Numinous Treasure Jade Casket Luminous Perfection Great Fast and Repentance Ritual*. One fascicle. Compiled by Du Guangting of the late Tang and Five Dynasties period.

**Source Text:** *Daoist Canon of the Zhengtong Era*, *Dongxuan Section*, *Weiyi* (Ritual Decorum) Category.

**The Supreme Numinous Treasure Jade Casket Luminous Perfection Great Fast and Repentance Ritual**
Compiled by Du Guangting, Master of Broad Achievement

---

**East**
We, your servants and assembly, now kindle incense in reverent devotion, bowing in gratitude to the Supreme Numinous Treasure Heavenly Worthy of the Infinite East, the perfected and sagely hosts of attained Daoist deities, the Most High Lord of the Three Heavens of Supreme Purity and Primordial Infinity, the Supreme Elder Lord and August Sovereign Lord Lao, the August Sovereign Elder and Azure Spirit Supreme Perfected Heavenly Emperor, the Heavenly Emperor Elder and Nine Elders of the Immortal Metropolis, the Nine Elder Lords and the myriad-fold qi of the Dao with its one thousand two hundred officials, the Azure Florescence Great Divinity of the Jade Terrace of Supreme Purity and the Eastern Pole Elder, the Prime Minister Sima Azure Youth, the Golden Portal Posterior Sage Emperor, the True Yang Primordial Azure Spirit and the Nine Immortals of Numinous Treasure, the Azure Spirit Jade Maidens and the Four Perfected of the Host Immortals, and all the revered numina of the Infinite Eastern Realm.

We repent for all transgressions and errors of our past lives and present existence. Through this supplication and gratitude, we beseech their complete eradication, that our essence be refined in azure purity and our spirits ascend to the azure firmament. May we attain the ranks of the perfected, bestowing blessings upon the imperial enterprise. May the dynastic fortune endure and the sagely person flourish for ten thousand years. May all under heaven and the four seas together enjoy great peace. After attaining the Dao, may we ascend into formlessness and unite with the Dao in truth.

---

**South**
We, your servants and assembly, now kindle incense in reverent devotion, bowing in gratitude to the Supreme Numinous Treasure Heavenly Worthy of the Infinite South, the perfected and sagely hosts of attained Daoist deities, the Infinite Great Dao’s Southern Supreme Vermilion Emperor and Cinnabar Spirit Elder Lord, the Jade Maiden of Grand Harmony and Longevity Minister Sima, the Great Divinity of Life-Preservation Han the Elder, the Southern Pole Lord of Salvation, the Flying Heavenly Spirits and the Immortals of Liberation and Rebirth, the Jade Calendar Inscribers of Life, the Lords of Myriad Salvations and Ten Thousand Lives, and all the revered numina of the Infinite Southern Realm.

We repent for all transgressions and errors of our past lives and present existence. Through this repentance and gratitude, we beseech their complete eradication. May our essence be polished upon the cinnabar mounds and our spirits dwell in the crimson palaces. May we wander the celestial paths and repose in the cloud thoroughfares. May we ascend to the ranks of the lofty perfected, bestowing auspiciousness upon great enterprises. May the imperial enterprise be securely established and the sagely longevity far-reaching. May the hidden and manifest multitudes of beings all ascend to the realm of longevity. After attaining the Dao, may we ascend into formlessness and unite with the Dao in truth.

---

**West**
We, your servants and assembly, now kindle incense in reverent devotion, bowing in gratitude to the Supreme Numinous Treasure Heavenly Worthy of the Infinite West, the perfected and sagely hosts of attained Daoist deities, the Infinite Great Dao’s Supreme Emperor of the Western Florescence and Supreme Ultimate, the Jade Perfected of the Golden Hall and the Immortal Mother of Tortoise Terrace, the Posterior Sage Emperor and Prime Minister of the Golden Portal, the Primordial Heavenly Master and Lord of Myriad Sages, the Perfected of the Western Pole and Master of All Immortals, the Jade Maidens and Perfected of the Western Spirit, and all the revered numina of the Infinite Western Realm.

We repent for all transgressions and errors from kalpas without beginning, through past lives and the present existence. Through this repentance and gratitude, we beseech their complete eradication. May we refine our luminous forms in the jade capital and congeal our truth in primordial simplicity. May we ascend to the fruition of the Dao, perpetually blessing the imperial abode. May the dynastic fortune be as steadfast as Mount Song and longevity as boundless as the seas. May the myriad regions submit to transformation and the eight directions share a common culture. May the state prosper, the people rejoice, and all attain the realm of blessings. After attaining the Dao, may we ascend into formlessness and unite with the Dao in truth.

---

**North**
We, your servants and assembly, now kindle incense in reverent devotion, bowing in gratitude to the Supreme Numinous Treasure Heavenly Worthy of the Infinite North, the perfected and sagely hosts of attained Daoist deities, the Infinite Great Dao’s Most High Lord Lao, the Supreme Elder and August Jade Dawn Lord, the Northern Pole Perfected Duke and Lord of Myriad Sages, the Supreme Yin Perfected, the Wondrous Conduct Perfected, the Lord of Life and Death Registers of the Supreme Yin, the Minister of Calculations and Great Divinity of Life and Death, the August Perfected of the Supreme Truth, the Jade Maidens and Immortals, and all the true numina of the Infinite Northern Realm.

We repent for the endless kalpas of our existences, abandoning and assuming bodies, bound by the five desires and six emotions, the four dusts and three karmas, never free from accumulated defilements, entangled in karmic conditions. Through this repentance and gratitude, we beseech their complete eradication. May we transcend the hidden yin and ascend to the celestial realms. May we attain the fruition of the perfected, bestowing blessings upon the sagely enterprise. May heavenly longevity be extended and the imperial foundation flourish eternally. May the hidden and manifest share in blessings, and all beings, moving and still, partake in glory. May all within the eight directions together attain the radiant transformation. After attaining the Dao, may we ascend into formlessness and unite with the Dao in truth.

**Northeast**

We now reverently burn incense, humbly invoking and offering gratitude to the **Northeast**, to the **Limitless Supreme Numinous Treasure Heavenly Worthy**, the perfected assembly of great sagely and most true divine beings, the **Limitless Dao’s Flying Heavenly Divine Kings**, the **Eradicating Demons and Governing Spirits Worthy Marshal**, the **Divine Sovereign of Myriad Salvations and Myriad Births**, the **Supreme Mysterious Elder Lord**, the perfected ones and jade maidens, and all divine spirits of the **Northeast’s Limitless Realm**.

We repent for the boundless kalpas since time immemorial, in past lives and this present existence, for the three karmas and ten entanglements, the six emotions and four great elements, our ingrained habits clinging like fetters, drifting and wandering endlessly. Through this repentance and gratitude, we beseech their complete eradication. May we cross the ford of the five burdens and be stricken from the ledgers of the nine darknesses. May we ascend the cloud paths of the divine, soaring upon the celestial thoroughfares. Refined and transformed by nature, may we roam freely in eternal joy. May auspicious blessings flow to the imperial lineage, bestowing prosperity upon the emperor’s person. May we lead the realm into great peace, dispelling the gloom of the hidden realms. May the Dao’s winds spread afar, and wisdom’s grace suffuse all. Together may we bathe in the mysterious repose, forever attaining vast fortune. After attaining the Dao, may we ascend into formlessness and unite with the Dao in truth.

**Southeast**

We now reverently burn incense, humbly invoking and offering gratitude to the **Southeast**, to the **Limitless Supreme Numinous Treasure Heavenly Worthy**, the perfected assembly of great sagely and most true divine beings, the **Southern Elder Lord, Cinnabar Spirit August Elder, Supreme Prime Perfected of Long Life**, the **Marshals of Salvation, Lifespan, and Records**, the **Life-Nurturing Lord Han and Venerable Master**, the **Perfected of Reviving the Dead and Restoring Life**, the **Great Divinity of Refining Bones and Restoring Form**, the **Jade Maidens of Supreme Harmony**, the **Perfected of Myriad Blessings**, the **Great Sagely Flying Heavenly Divine Kings of the Southeast**, and all divine spirits of the **Southeast’s Limitless Realm**.

We repent for all kalpas since the beginning, up to this present moment, for abandoning and assuming bodies, for all past errors and transgressions. Through this repentance and gratitude, we beseech their complete eradication. May the smoke of defilements and afflictions dissipate, and body and mind become pure as clouds. May we refine our forms in the cinnabar radiance, ascending to the ranks of the azure splendor. Following the true Dao of the Eight Emperors, may we ascend to the nine heavens in a mysterious assembly. May auspicious blessings and great fortune flow forth, eternally securing the imperial foundation. May the imperial enterprise flourish and the cosmos rest in tranquility. May the nine darknesses and six destinies, the five sufferings and three paths, all transcend the bonds of karmic opposition and together realize the joy of carefree freedom. After attaining the Dao, may we ascend into formlessness and unite with the Dao in truth.

**Southwest**

We now reverently burn incense, humbly invoking and offering gratitude to the **Southwest**, to the **Limitless Supreme Numinous Treasure Heavenly Worthy**, the perfected assembly of great sagely and most true divine beings, the **Heavenly August Elder of the Southwest’s Limitless Primordial August Perfected Lord**, the **Cavern Spirit’s Great Yang Assembly of Divine Life-Nurturing Lords**, the **Flying Heavenly Great Divinities of Long Life and Salvation**, the perfected ones and jade maidens, and all divine spirits of the **Southwest’s Limitless Realm**.

We repent for the countless kalpas since the ancestral shrines’ venerated spirits, from the beginningless past to this present moment, for abandoning and assuming bodies, for all past errors and transgressions. Through this repentance and gratitude, we beseech their complete eradication. May we refine our souls in the mysterious yin and ascend our spirits to the cinnabar palaces. May we wander and feast upon the Crimson Mound, soaring and flying over the Great Desert. May the nine sages ride abreast, and the seven perfected drive together. May we bestow auspicious blessings upon the phoenix towers, extending the great enterprise. May the emperor’s longevity rival the five mountains, and the imperial sway spread over the ten thousand lands. May the hidden realms open to light, and all beings bask in clarity. After attaining the Dao, may we ascend into formlessness and unite with the Dao in truth.

**Northwest**

We now reverently burn incense, humbly invoking and offering gratitude to the **Northwest**, to the **Limitless Supreme Numinous Treasure Heavenly Worthy**, the perfected assembly of great sagely and most true divine beings, the **True August Elder of the Great Dao’s Supreme Emperor**, the **Most True Limitless Flying Heavenly Divine Kings**, the **Supreme Sagely Divinities of Salvation and Numinous Treasure**, the perfected ones and jade maidens, and all venerated spirits of the **Northwest’s Limitless Realm**.

We repent for all transgressions, boundless and immeasurable, from the beginningless past to this day. Through this repentance and gratitude, we beseech their complete eradication. May we uplift our essence from the hidden barriers and ascend our spirits to the celestial palaces. Riding the jade chariots, may we attain the ranks of the flying jade. Thus may we join the ancestral spirits of the true lineage, enduring as long as heaven. May the great enterprise of heaven and earth embody the Dao and endure forever. May the emperor’s longevity rival the five mountains, and the imperial way spread over the ten thousand lands. May all living beings enjoy peace, and the living and dead rest in tranquility. May all sentient beings together attain the realm of blessings. After attaining the Dao, may we ascend into formlessness and unite with the Dao in truth.

**Upper Realm**

We now reverently burn incense, humbly invoking and offering gratitude to the **Upper Realm**, to the **Limitless Supreme Numinous Treasure Heavenly Worthy**, the perfected assembly of great sagely and most true divine beings, the **Supreme Three Venerables of the Limitless Great Dao**, the **Thirty-Six Heavenly Emperors**, the **Supreme Lord of the Nameless and Formless Dao of Limitless Clarity We now confess: from the beginningless past until this very day, our transgressions and errors have been measureless and boundless. Through this repentance and offering, we humbly beseech their complete eradication. May we depart the courts of endless night and enter the radiant precincts of light. May the Ten Perfected Ones vouchsafe our ascent, and the Five Thearchs unseal the gates of clarity. May we bathe in the pools of flowing fire and ascend the towers of Cavernous Yang. May we transcend the nether prisons and soar to the celestial capitals. Riding the eight winds in circling flight, we trace the nine clouds in joyous wandering. Above, we present ourselves at the Grand Ultimate, our names enrolled in the Purple Clarity. May blessings descend upon our descendants’ counsels, and our great foundations endure through ten thousand generations. May darkness and light unfold in harmony, and seas and mountains rise in peace. May all sentient beings enter the realm of longevity.

After attaining the Dao, we shall ascend into the formless and unite with the Dao in truth.

Now, with reverence, we burn incense and prostrate ourselves in homage to the **Limitless Supreme Numinous Treasure Heavenly Worthy of the Lower Realm**, the **Perfected and Divine Hosts of Attained Dao**, the **Great Sages and Venerable Spirits of the Limitless Dao of the Lower Realm’s Mysterious Yellow Cavernous Abyss**, the **August High Sovereign of the Nine Lands and Four Offices**, the **Five Thearchs**, the **Twelve Immortal Lords**, the **Four Offices’ Perfected Ones of the Great Numinous Yellow Mother**, the **Central Yellow Queen of the Nine Earths**, the **Perfected Ones of the Nine Palaces**, the **Eight Scribes and Nine Mysterious Lords**, the **Twelve Auxiliary Ministers of the Four Dignitaries and Five Marchmounts**, the **Deputy Stewards of the Nine Rivers and Eight Seas**, the **Thirty-Six Grotto-Heavens**, the **Ten Great Grotto-Heavens**, the **Seventy-Two Blessed Lands**, the **Three Hundred Sixty Sacred Mountains**, **Kunlun and Everlasting Yang**, **Vast Mulberry Fields and Boundless Wilds**, **Luminous Farmer and Earth’s Axis**, the **Perfected Divine Immortals**, the **Water Thearchs of the Nine Rivers and Twelve Stream Sources**, the **Spiritual Caverns and Marshes**, and all **Venerable Perfected Ones**.

We confess: from kalpas innumerable until this day, our transgressions and errors have been measureless and boundless. Through this repentance and offering, we humbly beseech their complete eradication. May our earthly records be cast aside in the nether courts, and may the radiance of Yang suffuse the cinnabar palaces. May the sun’s rays stream forth, refining our essence and tempering our spirits. Riding the swift gales of the Three Simplicities, we mount the flying canopies of the Eight Phoenixes. Ascending to the true celestial realms, we feast in the imperial homeland of light. We summon the Five Elders to the august palace and present ourselves at the Three Clarities’ purple towers. Truly, we ascend to the true radiance, gathering in the jeweled capital. May vast blessings flow to the imperial person, and may the imperial foundation stand firm for a hundred million years. May the springs’ gates open in brightness, and all beings awaken to renewal. May the winds of virtue and power ever flourish, and may prosperity’s blessings endure forever. May the Five Marchmounts stand in stillness, and the Eight Seas settle in calm. May all under heaven universally receive the mysterious blessings.

After attaining the Dao, we shall ascend into the formless and unite with the Dao in truth.
