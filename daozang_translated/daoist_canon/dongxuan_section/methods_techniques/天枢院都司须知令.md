**Heavenly Pivot Bureau Chief Administrator’s Essential Decrees**

The Heavenly Pivot Director-General is a living administrator in the world, overseeing seven Recording Clerks and seven Bureau Assistants, who receive and dispatch matters.

The seven Recording Clerks command the *Primordial Assembly Camp*, a force of ten thousand soldiers known as the *Heaven-Sustaining Army*.

**Receiving Clerks:**
1. **First Recording Clerk Bureau**, *Paulownia-Bearing Perfected* Shen Quan.
2. **Second Recording Clerk Bureau**, *Flourishing Integrity Perfected* Wang Xiang.
3. **Third Recording Clerk Bureau**, *Yang Brightness Immortal Aide* Fu Shou.
4. **Fourth Recording Clerk Bureau**, *Illuminating Salvation and Dispelling Debts Perfected* Feng Xiu.
5. **Fifth Recording Clerk Bureau**, *Unassigned Immortal Rank* Hu Zhu.
6. **Sixth Recording Clerk Bureau**, *Unassigned Immortal Rank* Kong You.
7. **Seventh Recording Clerk Bureau**, *Unassigned Immortal Rank* Fang Zheng.

Beyond managing the seventy-four bureaus’ official matters, the *Stipend and Lifespan Bureau* serves as a subordinate office, attached to the First Recording Clerk Bureau, jointly overseeing the seventy-four bureaus’ signings.

The seven Recording Clerks are also living administrators in the world, subordinate to the Director-General, their affairs concealed and not transmitted.
