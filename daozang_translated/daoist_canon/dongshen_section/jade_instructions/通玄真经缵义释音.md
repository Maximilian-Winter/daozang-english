# 通玄真经缵义释音

**Collected Exegesis and Phonetic Glosses of the *Tongxuan Zhenjing*
Volume One: "Dao’s Origin"**

*Gǔ* (汨): pronounced *mì*. Meaning "to flow and circulate."
*Dié* (墆): pronounced *dié*. Meaning "to halt."
*Dú* (殰): pronounced *dú*. Meaning "fetal corruption."
*Qū* (诎): pronounced *qū*, synonymous with "to bend."
*Gǔ* (滑): pronounced *mì*.
*Shī* (湿): pronounced *shī*.
*Xié* (邪): pronounced *xié*.
*Zhàn* (湛): pronounced *chén*. Meaning "to drown."
*Wǎn* (惋): pronounced *wǎn*. Meaning "to sigh in astonishment."
*Jiǎ* (嘏): pronounced *jiǎ*. Meaning "great."
*Sì* (汜): pronounced *fàn*.
*Zǐ* (訾): pronounced *zǐ*. Meaning "indistinct in content."
*Qí-Náo* (蚑蛲): upper *qí* (岐), lower *náo* (饒). A type of small insect.
*Chuāng* (创): pronounced *chuāng*.
*Nào* (淖): pronounced *nào*. Meaning "harmonious."
*Cuì* (毳): pronounced *chuì-ruì*. Meaning "gentle in intent."
*Dǎn* (亶): pronounced *duō-hàn*. Meaning "trustworthy."
*Cǎn* (憯): pronounced *cǎn*. Meaning "venomous."
*Fú* (枹): pronounced *fú*. Meaning "drumstick."
*Diān* (蹎): pronounced *diān*. Meaning "to walk slowly."
*Míng* (暝): pronounced *mián*. Meaning "to gaze with eyes level."
*Kē* (轲): pronounced *kě*. Meaning "a demeanor of discontent."
**Volume Two: "Essence and Sincerity"**
*Pù* (暴): pronounced *pù*.
*Jué* (谲): pronounced *jué*. Meaning "deceitful."
*Jià* (贾): pronounced *jià*. Meaning "to calculate."
*Shì* (螫): pronounced *shì*.

**Of Insect Poison.** *Chóng dú* (虫毒): insect venom.

*Miù* (缪): pronounced *miào*. Same meaning.

*Fǎng yáng tǎng yáng* (仿佯倘徉): synonymous.

*Líng yǔ* (囹圄): upper *líng* (零), lower *yǔ* (语). Name of a prison.

*Yǎn* (噞): pronounced *yú-jiǎn*. Fish drinking water.

*Xiào hū* (呼): upper *xiào* (叫), lower *hū* (去声). To cry out loudly.

*Jǐng* (景): pronounced *yǐng*. "Form" and "shadow" share the same meaning.

*Chuí* (倕): pronounced *chuí*. Craftsman *Gōngshū* (工输).

*Wáng* (亡): pronounced *wú*. Same meaning.

*Qūn* (囷): pronounced *qū-lún*. Round granary.

**Volume Three: "Nine Safeguards"**

*Biàn níng* (便宁): pronounced *pí-lián*. Meaning "peaceful."

*Yǐng zhù* (影柱): to be as if absent. Not ensnared by things.

*Jiàn gǔ* (建鼓): to be as if empty. Not moved by sound.

*Qiān* (攓): pronounced *jiǎn*. Same meaning.

*Jié* (): pronounced *jié*. Same meaning.

*Yòu zhī* (侑卮): upper *yòu* (宥). A tilting vessel.

*Dēng jiǎ* (登假): lower *xiá* (遐). Same meaning.

**Volume Four: "Tallying Words"**

*Bèi* (倍): pronounced *bèi*. Same meaning.

*Yāo* (要): pronounced *yāo*. The world in pairs, both grasped. He who grasps both is a hegemon.

*Qū* (屈): pronounced *jiǔ-wù*. Meaning "tortuous."

*Shè* (慑): pronounced *zhé*.

*Yù* (愈): pronounced *yù*.

**Volume Five: "Virtue and the Dao"**

*Jī* (稽): to gather. *Xù* (蓄): same meaning.

*Cì* (刺): pronounced *qī-cì*.

*Jià biàn* (贾便): upper *gǔ* (古), lower *pián* (平声).

*Rú* (蠕): pronounced *ruǎn*. *Qiú lóu* (蝼): an insect. *Yuān* (蜎): pronounced *yuān*. A flying insect.

*Guì* (刿): pronounced *miào-wèi*. Meaning "harmless."

*Zuó* (捽): pronounced *cún-wù*. Meaning "to drag."

*Qù* (趣): pronounced *qū*.

*Jí* (亟): pronounced *qù-lì*. Meaning "swift." *Pí* (罢): pronounced *pí*.

**Scroll Seven: "Subtle Brightness"**

*Pēng*: pronounced *fū*. The sound of a creaking cart.

*Qiāo*: upper tone *hé*, lower tone *qiào*. Fierce and urgent.

*Chēng*: pronounced *chēng*. To lift and extol.

*Xí*: pronounced *xí*. A male shaman is called *xí*; a female, *wū*.

*Xù*: pronounced *xū-jù*. Warm qi nurturing things.
