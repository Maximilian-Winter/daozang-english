# 北斗七元金玄羽章

**The Golden Mysterious Feathered Chapters of the Seven Primes of the Northern Dipper**

*Scripture Title:* The Golden Mysterious Feathered Chapters of the Seven Primes of the Northern Dipper. Author unknown, approximately dating to the Song dynasty. It contains hymns in praise of the Seven Primes of the Northern Dipper and the two auxiliary stars, the Left and Right Assistants. Compared to another version, it includes two additional chapters for the auxiliary stars but lacks talismanic incantations and esoteric instructions. One fascicle. Base text from: *Daoist Canon of the Zhengtong Era*, section on Hymns and Eulogies of the *Dongshen* Division.

---

**The Golden Mysterious Feathered Chapters of the Seven Primes of the Northern Dipper**
*(Version Without Talismanic Incantations)*

The True Lord of Life-Registration of the Eastern Dipper,
The True Lord of Birth-Registration of the Southern Dipper,
The True Lord of Name-Registration of the Western Dipper,
The True Lord of Death-Registration of the Northern Dipper,
The True Lord of the Grand Tenuity of the Central Dipper,
The True Lord of Luminous Virtue and Kindly Life,
The True Lord of Penetrating Clarity and Myriad Blessings,
The True Lord of Light-Shedding and Longevity-Prolonging,
The True Lord of Gentle Virtue and Lifespan-Enhancing,
The True Lord of Origin-Preserving and Life-Registering,
The True Lord of Heavenly Virtue and Goodness-Registering.

---

**First Mysterious Chapter: The Star Official of Greedy Wolf**
The Northern Dipper prolongs life,
Reverting to the Four Numinous Ones in truth.
Its flowing radiance shines downward,
Illuminating the dark and profound.
The Seven Primes record the registers,
Names inscribed upon the Purple Jade.
It preserves the body and extends life,
Ever prosperous and upright.
Ears and eyes grow keen and bright,
The hun and po abide in lasting peace.
It nurtures and cultivates essence and qi,
Shining together with the spirits.
Life is prolonged naturally,
Ascending to the Jade Clarity.

---

**Second Mysterious Chapter: The Star Official of Great Gate**
The Northern Dipper safeguards destiny,
Mysterious numina arrayed in display.
The Cinnabar Field flows unimpeded,
Its movements harmonizing with the immortals.
The Seven Primes fix the registers,
Names inscribed upon the Purple Script.
It guards the body and registers longevity,
Fortune and blessings ever renewed.
Banishing evil and malevolence,
Auspicious omens daily increase.
Coming and going without hindrance,
Revolving together with the spirits.
Destiny is preserved naturally,
Ascending to the Jade Palace.

---

**Third Mysterious Chapter: The Star Official of Emolument Storage**
The Northern Dipper dispels calamity,
Lofty immortals and supreme numina.
Its flowing radiance shines universally,
Within and without, all is solemn and pure.
The Seven Primes record the registers,
Names inscribed upon the Purple Court.
It supports the body and extends life,
Emolument and rank ever flourishing.
The viscera grow clear and bright,
Movement and stillness alike at peace.
Light and fortune merge with the luminous,
Flourishing together with the spirits.
Calamity is dispelled naturally,
Ascending to the Jade Capital.

---

**Fourth Mysterious Chapter: The Star Official of Literary Brilliance**
The Northern Dipper dissolves misfortune,
Mysterious Yellow opens the yang.
The immortal numina manifest their shadows,
Bright and penetrating in the ten directions.
The Seven Primes hold the registers,
Names inscribed upon the Purple Chamber.
It guards the body and enhances life,
Ever inaugurating fortune and auspiciousness.
The Illuminated Perfected descend with blessings,
Washing away inauspiciousness and calamity.
Essence and qi of splendor congeal,
Dwelling together with the spirits.
Misfortune is dissolved naturally,
Ascending to the Jade Hall.

---

**Fifth Mysterious Chapter: The Star Official of Integrity and Chastity**
The Northern Dipper sustains the declining,
The True Ancestor descends with numina.
Flowing rosy clouds scatter their light,
Immortal retinues intermingle in abundance.
The Seven Primes govern the registers,
Names inscribed upon the Purple Encampment.
It fortifies the body and nurtures life,
Blessings converge in unbroken succession.
The Lofty Mysterious One receives and guides,
Wandering freely through the Five Clarities.
Riding the luminous scene in flight,
Ageless together with the spirits.
The declining is sustained naturally,
Ascending to the Jade Court.

---

**Sixth Mysterious Chapter: The Star Official of Martial Brilliance**
The Northern Dipper disperses disaster,
Mysterious radiance riding the void.
Numinous might stands guard in full,
Demonic and perverse traces vanish.
The Seven Primes register the records,
Names inscribed upon the Purple Vermilion.
It glorifies the body and increases life,
Enjoying boundless fortune.
Immortal officials support and protect,
Treading upon the clouds.
The Orthodox Perfected descend and gather,
Wise together with the spirits.
Disaster is dispersed naturally,
Ascending to the Jade Palace.

---

**Seventh Mysterious Chapter: The Star Official of Army Breaking**
The Northern Dipper enhances lifespan,
Luminous rosy clouds scatter and spread.
Flowing light universally enfolds,
True qi ever sustains.
The Seven Primes cross the registers,
Names inscribed upon the Purple Writ.
It governs the body and adds to life,
Ever dwelling in joy.
Flying immortals dance,
Feasting and roaming in the Mysterious Capital.
Penetrating vision within and without,
Dwelling together with the spirits.
Lifespan is enhanced naturally,
Ascending to the Jade Chariot.

---

**Mysterious Chapter of the Star Official of the Left Assistant**
Heaven and Earth intermingle in harmony,
The Three Destinies connect with the numinous.
Ascending to the celestial lodgings,
Flying through the Nine Stars.
In the left hand, the Hidden Writ;
In the right, the Feathered Scripture.
Bowing in audience before the August Emperor,
Receiving the name of the Dao.
Surpassing the Floral Canopy,
Soaring in the Purple Court.
Today, practicing the Dao,
Myriad blessings converge.
Receiving towering fortune,
Forever enjoying long life.
The body transforms into feathers,
Flying and ascending to the Supreme Clarity.

---

**Mysterious Chapter of the Star Official of the Right Assistant**
The Dao harmonizes with the Three Subtleties,
The Mysterious Terrace elevates the true.
Exiting the constant and entering the void,
Escaping form at the Celestial Pass.
Floating and soaring through the Eight Extremities,
Riding the luminous scene and purple mist.
Treading the Nine Qi in flight,
Transforming in countless millions.
Roaming beneath the Northern Canopy,
Whistling and chanting at the Curved Array.
What is sought is as desired,
What is petitioned is as spoken.
Ever presiding over Heaven and Earth,
Flourishing dawns preserve the years.

---

**The Golden Mysterious Feathered Chapters of the Seven Primes of the Northern Dipper concludes.**
