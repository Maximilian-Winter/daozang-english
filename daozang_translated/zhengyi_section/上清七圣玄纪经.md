# 上清七圣玄纪经

**The Supreme Clarity Scripture of the Seven Sages’ Mysterious Records**

**Scripture Title:** *The Supreme Clarity Scripture of the Seven Sages’ Mysterious Records*. Also known as *The Transmission of the Eight Perfected and Seven Sages*. Author unknown, approximately composed during the Eastern Jin or Southern Dynasties. Originally in seven scrolls, only a fragmentary catalog remains.

**Source:** *Zhengtong Daoist Canon*, Orthodox Section, Part One.

---

**Transmission of the Supreme Clarity Grand Dao Lord of the Great Dao**
The first record, titled *The Supreme Scripture of the Eight Simplicities* or *The Hidden Writ of the Supreme Lord*, also called *The Golden Perfection and Jade Radiance of the Effulgent Seven Origins* or *The Flying Scripture of the Eight Luminaries*. Whoever possesses it becomes a divine immortal; whoever practices it becomes a perfected person.

**Transmission of the Purple Clarity Grand Simplicity Lord of the Three Origins**
The second record, titled *The Scripture of the Seven Transformations of Essence and Light in the Grotto of Reality*, also called *The Supreme Scripture of the Flying Radiance of the Primordial Numinous Treasure* or *The Hidden Writ of the Three Origins*. Whoever possesses it becomes a divine immortal; whoever practices it becomes a perfected person.

**Transmission of the White Jade Tortoise Terrace Nine Phoenixes Grand Perfected Queen Mother of the West**
The third record, titled *The Supreme Scripture of the Mysterious Records in the Grotto of the Numinous*, also called *The Scripture of the Great Possession and Wondrous Mystery*. Whoever possesses it becomes a divine immortal; whoever practices it becomes a perfected person.

**Transmission of the Central Yellow Elder Lord**
The fourth record, titled *The Central Scripture of the Nine Perfected*, also called *The Scripture of Flying Feathers*. Whoever possesses it ascends in broad daylight; whoever practices it becomes a perfected person.

**Transmission of the Great Emperor Lord of Fusang**
The fifth record, titled *The Hidden Writ of the Great Empyrean*, also called *The Scripture of Jade Pendants and Golden Circlets*. Whoever possesses it attains long life and commands spirits; whoever practices it becomes a perfected person.

**Transmission of the Golden Portal Sage Lord**
The sixth record, titled *The Numinous Writ of the Purple Text*, also called *The Treasure Scripture of the Five Elders*. Whoever possesses it undergoes liberation in death; whoever practices it becomes a perfected person.

**Transmission of the Ninefold Subtlety Grand Perfected Jade Guardian, Golden Portal Supreme Minister, Grand Director of Destinies, High Dawn Master, Azure Lad of the Eastern Sea**
The seventh record, titled *The Scripture of the Grotto Observation of the Jade Guardian*, also called *The Supreme Scripture of the Numinous Clouds*. Whoever possesses it undergoes liberation in death in broad daylight; whoever practices it becomes a perfected person.

The seven transmissions of the sages: whoever possesses all of them becomes a perfected person, and the host of spirits attend their chambers, journeying through the Jade Clarity.

**The Eight Perfected:**
- Eastern Pole Perfected, Lingyang Ziming
- Southern Pole Perfected, Master Red Pine
- Western Pole Perfected, Wang Fangping
- Northern Pole Perfected, Anqi Sheng
- [Missing character] Cheng Zigao
- Supreme Clarity Immortal King, Zhao Chezi
- Nine Palaces Supreme Minister, Master Changli
- Supreme Ultimate Left Duke, Master Beiluo

**The Two Worthy Perfected:**
- Master Azure Radiance
- Master Gu Xi

Whoever possesses these becomes a Perfected of the Nine Palaces, not merely an immortal.

**Here ends *The Supreme Clarity Scripture of the Seven Sages’ Mysterious Records*.**
